Rules quick reference
Points (10 per match): 1 pt per hole low net · tied hole = 0.5 each · +1 pt team low net · tied low net = 0.5 each
Handicap: Tracked separately for front & back nine · requires 2 rounds on each side to establish · rolling last 4 rounds, drop highest, average 3 lowest, multiply diff by 0.9, round at .5, max 7 · no strokes on par 3s
No-show / bye: Ghost score = last-place team's most recent round on that side (uses next-to-last if that team is the no-show)
Skins: $18 pot · whole field competes every hole · net score wins hole · ties carry forward · payout rounds DOWN to nearest $0.50 · remainder rolls to next week
